Starfish Cuatro Palmas is an all-inclusive, adults-only retreat nestled along the sandy beachfront of Varadero, Cuba. Guests will immediately be drawn to the picturesque surroundings patio courtyard, red roofs, and tropical gardens. Starfish Cuatro Palmas offers a vibrant atmosphere, quality accommodations, 24 hour food and beverage service, sparkling swimming pools, organized games, a fitness facility, and a variety of fun activities.

At Starfish Resorts, you can rest assured you will enjoy friendly and personable service, clean and spacious rooms, international culinary classics, one-of-a-kind entertainment, and an authentic experience for guests of all ages. Perfect for families, couples, and singles on a quest for the perfect all-inclusive vacation, Starfish Resorts believes that #YouAreTheStar, and that you deserve to shine bright.
